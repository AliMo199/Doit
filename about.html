Our Humble Project

DOIT

DOIT - A to-do list project designed to help users efficiently organize, prioritize, and track their tasks, enhancing productivity with a user-friendly interface and customizable features.

Create & Organize Tasks

This feature enables users to create detailed, prioritized to-do lists with task names, descriptions, categories, comments, and deadlines for efficient daily planning and organization.

Completion & Deletion

This feature allows users to mark tasks as done, move them to a separate list, or delete them with confirmation prompts to maintain a clutter-free, productive, and organized workspace.

Support Different Themes

Dark mode support provides a customizable, eye-friendly alternative theme that reduces brightness, enhances comfort in low-light environments, and can extend battery life on OLED devices.

About

Inspired by the complications of existing to-do list apps, I developed DOIT, a simple, intuitive, and registration-free to-do list application, combining my computer science background and passion for creating practical, user-centric solutions.
